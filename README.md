# Project-Milestone

# Option 1: Film and Color (connection to thesis)
Since my thesis is related to film and color, I am interested in exploring datasets related to movies, such as metadata or image/color analysis.

Data: Movie datasets (IMDb)
Type: Mixed (text + numerical + possibly image data)
Size: LARGE
Pre-processing: Cleaning text data, extracting relevant features, computing color distributions from images

Possible questions:
How does color usage vary across films or time periods?
Are there patterns between genre and color?
Can we cluster films based on visual or descriptive features?

# Option 2: Wind Energy in Chile
I am interested in exploring data about wind farms in Chile. This could include their location, energy production, number of turbines, and distribution by region.

Data: Government or open energy datasets and The Wind Power Database
Type: Structured data (tables with regions, coordinates, capacity, etc.)
Size: Medium (depends on the dataset)
Pre-processing: Cleaning location names, possibly combining multiple datasets (e.g., energy production + geographic data)

Possible questions:
How are wind farms distributed across the country?
Which regions produce the most energy?
Is there a relationship between geography and energy production?
